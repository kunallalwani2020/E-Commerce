Electronic commerce or e-commerce is a term for any kind of business or commercial transaction, which involves the transfer of information on the Internet. It covers a number of different types of businesses, from retail to consumers through an auction or music sites, to business exchanges trading goods and services between corporations. Now e-commerce becomes one of the most important aspects of the Internet to appear.

E-commerce allows consumers to communicate electronically for goods and services without the barriers of time and distance. E-commerce is growing rapidly in the past five years and is expected to continue at this pace or even accelerate. In the near future the boundaries between “conventional” and “electronic” commerce will become increasingly blurred as more and more businesses move sections of their operations onto the Internet.

E-commerce has allowed consumers to make transactions from the comfort of their own home, transactions that usually occurs in the walls of the store. Transactions are now only takes a few minutes instead of hours involved in physically getting to the stores.
